About Us

The Hamburger Society is a community of food lovers in search of the best food, specifically hamburgers Zagreb has to offer. It is a community brought together by their love of good food, good company and good times. We offer general information on where you can get the best burgers in Zagreb, but not only burgers, this is about making friends and eating yummy food.
